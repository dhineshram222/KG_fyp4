A local area network is a computer network that interconnects computers within a limited area. One is a wired LAN, another one is a wireless LAN. We have already seen in the previous lecture that Ethernet is used to connect two or more nodes. A wild area network is a telecommunications network that extends over a larger geographical area. Say for example, there is a guy in India who wants to send some data to a guy who is in Australia. Cloud computing means it is the on demand availability of computer resources. It means employees can bring their own device and connect it to their office network and still work with the office network. We can just store the data somewhere in a secure manner and get the data whenever we want it. A Local Area Network is a computer network that interconnects computers within a limited area such as a residence, school, laboratory, university campus or office building. A wide area network (WAN) is a telecommunications network that extends over a large geographical area for the primary purpose of computer networking.
