Levels of Data Abstraction is associated with Conceptual Level and View Level and Logical Level. View Level provides Multiple views of Database, and view Level is associated. Physical Level is Lowest level Levels of DataAbstraction and connects to Database. LogicalLevel deals with What Data Stored and Data Relationships. Abstract Data is Preferred over Complete Data. User- system Interaction uses Interfaces. At Conceptual level, server is At Conceptually Level and fetches. At View Level, user does not directly interact with Physical Level and And sends information to Server.
