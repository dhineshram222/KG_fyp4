In this presentation we will focus on the view of data. In the last presentation I told you we have multiple views at the presentation level. The answer for this will be revealed in this presentation. Before we step into various views of data we need to understand what is data abstraction. We know basically the primary goal of any database is to store and retrieve the data conveniently and effectively. And obviously we need storage for doing this and this storage may involve simple or even complex data structures. The logical level describes what data are actually to be stored in the databases. The physical level deals with how the data are stored and the relationship among the data. At the logical level, we use only simple data structures, but at the physical level we use complex data structures. It means it involves more complexity and we are going to hide most of the complexity.
