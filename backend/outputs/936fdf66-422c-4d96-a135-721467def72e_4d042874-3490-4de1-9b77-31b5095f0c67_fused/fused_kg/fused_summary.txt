Network Topology involves arrangement of Nodes. Can be viewed as Physical Topology and Logical Topology. Ring Topology is a type of Closed loop variation of Bus Topology, and has a Part Root Node. Tree Topology combines characteristics of Bus topology and Star Topology to create a tree topology. Also, central Node (Hub/Switch) provides Centralized administration for Star Topologies.
